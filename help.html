Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and get the support you need

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and BMP formats. For best results, use clear, well-lit images with the license plate clearly visible.

How accurate is the recognition?

Our system achieves over 95% accuracy with clear images. Results may vary based on image quality and lighting conditions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We never share your information with third parties.

How can I get support?

Our support team is available 24/7. Contact us through the form below or email support@licenseeye.com
